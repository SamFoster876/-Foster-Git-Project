Welcome to a Trip to Greece. Our website that will teach you all you need to know about Greece. You will learn about the History, the nature, the area and the people of Greece. Greece, located in southeastern Europe, is a nation steeped in rich history and cultural significance. Often looked at as the cradle of Western civilization, Greece has a legacy that spans thousands of years, making it one of the oldest civilizations in the world. Explore the cradle of Western civilization with a journey through Greece's ancient wonders. Marvel at the Acropolis in Athens, where the iconic Parthenon stands as a symbol of classical Greek architecture. Discover the ancient sanctuary of Delphi, once considered the center of the world, and visit the historic theater of Epidaurus.
